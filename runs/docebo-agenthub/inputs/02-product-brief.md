# Product Brief

Docebo AgentHub is an AI-powered platform launched in April 2026 that uses
agentic AI to transform enterprise knowledge into learning content.

## Key capabilities
- Connects to 20+ enterprise knowledge sources including Confluence, SharePoint,
  and Google Drive
- Lets organizations access and search information across multiple systems from a
  single interface
- Built on Docebo's acquisition of Zive, a German enterprise AI and knowledge
  platform

## Broader platform context
- **Docebo Skills Intelligence** (powered by 365Talents acquisition) — skills
  detection and validation
- **Docebo MCP Server** — connects Docebo data to AI tools like Claude, Copilot,
  and ChatGPT (public beta now, GA July 2026)
- **Docebo Companion** — embeds AI-powered learning into work systems
- **Docebo AI Tutor** — on-demand study assistant

## Company / commercial
- Publicly traded (NASDAQ: DCBO, TSX: DCBO)
- Targets mid-market to large enterprises
- Focuses on HR and L&D leaders
- Pricing not publicly disclosed

## Core integrations
Confluence, SharePoint, Google Drive, Slack, Microsoft Teams, and 15+ other
enterprise knowledge sources.

## Current limitations (noted)
- AI agents are not yet live ("coming this fall" 2026)
- Pricing not publicly disclosed
- New category not yet established / recognized
