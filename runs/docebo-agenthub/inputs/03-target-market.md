# Target Market

Mid-market to large enterprise HR and L&D teams, specifically organizations with
500+ employees that have fragmented enterprise knowledge across multiple systems.

## Primary industries
Technology, financial services, healthcare, manufacturing, professional services.

## Geography
North America and EMEA primarily.

## Company size focus
- Mid-market: 500–5,000 employees
- Enterprise: 5,000+ employees

## Buyer focus
HR and L&D leaders.
